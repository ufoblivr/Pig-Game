Welcome to Pig Game 🐷

The game is a simple two-player dice game called "Pig Game." The objective of the game is to be the first player to reach a score of 50 or more points. Players take turns rolling a dice and accumulating points. If a player rolls a 1, they lose their current score and the turn passes to the other player. If a player chooses to "Hold," their current score is added to their total score, and they pass the turn to the other player.
